Experience

Photogrammetry

While pursuing a Masters of Science, specializing in computer vision and 3D aerial surveying I have developed a passion for photogrammetry, particularily aerial photogrammetry. With a background in computer science it is very intersting to see the two methodologies of photogrammetry coming together and interweaving into new products. With the emergence of low cost aerial drones new avenues of surveying are be introduced and applied every year.


Software Development

After acquring an interest in software development in high school and pursuing a degree in computer science specializing in software design, programming is at the core of my interests. I have developed the skill of analyzing a task and being able to rapdily develop programs to automate the workflow, maximizing efficiency.
